Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09.09.2024

Welcome to GameHub! At GameHub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from www.gamehub.com (the “GameHub”).

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our Site, we may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign up or purchase a game, we collect your name, email address, payment information, and shipping details.
  • Payment Details: Your credit card or payment method details are securely processed by third-party payment processors.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Browsing Information: We collect information on how you use the Site, such as the pages viewed, links clicked, and time spent on each page.
  • Device Information: We collect data about the device you are using, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process transactions and deliver your purchased games.
  • Customer Service: To provide you with support and address any questions or concerns.
  • Marketing: To send you marketing communications if you have opted in to receive them.
  • Site Improvements: To analyze usage data and improve the overall functionality of our Site.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that help us provide services, such as payment processing, shipping, or marketing.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, we may disclose your personal information to comply with legal proceedings or protect our rights.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies allow us to recognize you when you return to our site and help us personalize content. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal or contractual obligations.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@gamehub.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to other websites not operated by GameHub. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites.

8. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the “Effective Date” at the top will be updated accordingly.

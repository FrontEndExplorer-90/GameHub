Cyberpunk: Rise of the Rebel

Platform: Playbox One X | Genre: Action-Adventure, RPG

Rating: 16+ | 4K Ultra HD, HDR Enhanced

Game Overview:

Step into a future where the line between humanity and technology is blurred, and the fight for survival is an everyday battle. Cyberpunk: Rise of the Rebel thrusts you into a neon-soaked, high-tech metropolis where corruption runs deep, and justice is a rare commodity. Play as a fierce and enigmatic protagonist, a combat specialist equipped with deadly skills and cutting-edge cybernetic enhancements. Her past is a mystery, her future uncertain, but one thing is clear: she’s on a mission to bring down those who have wronged her.

450,00 kr

Game Features:

  • Immersive Open World: Explore a sprawling cyberpunk city, from towering skyscrapers to the gritty underworld, teeming with secrets waiting to be uncovered.
  • Dynamic Combat System: Master the art of melee combat with your iconic cyber-sword, or upgrade your arsenal with high-tech weaponry and cybernetic abilities.
  • Deep Narrative: Unravel the protagonist’s mysterious past through a gripping storyline filled with betrayal, revenge, and redemption.
  • Hacker's Paradise: Dive into complex hacking puzzles, bypass security systems, and control enemy technology to turn the tide in your favour.
  • Customizable Upgrades: Enhance your character with powerful cybernetic upgrades, unlocking new abilities and combat moves tailored to your playstyle.
  • Stunning Visuals: Experience breath-taking 4K Ultra HD graphics, with HDR enhancements that bring the neon-lit cityscape to life like never before.

Storyline:

In a world where mega-corporations rule with an iron fist, one woman rises from the shadows to challenge the status quo. Betrayed and left for dead, she’s been rebuilt with cybernetic enhancements that make her more than human—but at what cost? As she delves deeper into the underbelly of the city, she must confront not only her enemies but the fragmented memories of a past she cannot fully remember. Will you help her reclaim her identity, or will the dark secrets of her past consume her? In Cyberpunk: Rise of the Rebel, every choice you make will shape her destiny and the future of the city itself.

Space War: Galactic Command

Platform: Playbox One X | Genre: Space Combat, Sci-Fi Strategy

Rating: 16+ | 4K Ultra HD, HDR Enhanced

Game Overview:

Enter the vast expanse of the cosmos in Space War: Galactic Command, a high-octane space combat game where the fate of the galaxy rests in your hands. As an ace pilot turned fleet commander, lead your forces through epic battles, strategic conquests, and critical missions to defeat a powerful enemy threatening the entire galaxy. Harness the advanced technology of your fleet, outmanoeuvre your enemies in intense dogfights, and make pivotal decisions that will shape the outcome of the war. With a deep narrative and stunning visuals, Space War: Galactic Command delivers a thrilling experience for any sci-fi or action enthusiast.

  • Strategic Warfare: Command your fleet, deploy tactics, and manage resources as you push back against enemy forces.
